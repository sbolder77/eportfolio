Induction Overview

Learning Outcomes

Explore technical and personal requirements of online learning. Competencies in tools available to online learners. Academic skills in writing, research, referencing, reflective learning and critical thinking at Postgraduate study. The induction was split into 2 weeks with several areas to explore to prepare for study.

Unit (Week) 1

Item Description
1 Welcome Session
2 An introduction to your subject area
3 Online Communities (Discussion Forum)
4 Equipment for my Course
5 Organising your learning
6 Support and Additional Information

Unit (Week) 2

Item Description
1 Academic Writing
2 Academic Research
3 Referencing your sources
4 Plagiarism
5 Induction Assignment

Artefacts

The main artefact of this induction module was a written essay on the benefits and importance of Postgraduate study in computer science.

Reflections

I have learnt how to adapt and change my writing style to adhere to academic standards. As i have not studied at undergraduate or postgraduate level before i have now gained an understanding of referencing and techniques. It has added an extra layer on top of my professional writing standards and given me extra insight into more formal written structures.
